Privacy & Independence

Your Data is Separate

We keep respondent identity separate from feedback. Institutions see aggregate results and published feedback, not your personal information.

Independent Platform

Ghana CX Index is not owned by any financial institution. We operate as an independent measurement authority focused on accountability and improvement.

Transparent Governance

Our complaint governance workflow is published. You can see exactly how feedback flows from respondent to institution to action.

Compliant Practices

We follow GDPR principles, obtain explicit consent, and publish our retention and use policies before launch.

Benchmarking Methodology (Summary)

The Ghana CX Index Score

  • Scale: 0–100 (higher is better)
  • Dimensions: 10 core CX dimensions, 7 questions each
  • Validity: A dimension is valid with minimum 5 numeric answers from 7
  • Calculation: (mean – 1) / 6 × 100
  • Overall Score: Average of valid dimensions; requires at least 8 valid dimensions
  • Benchmarks: Only shown when sample size ≥ 30 respondents
  • NPS: Separate calculation — % Promoters minus % Detractors
